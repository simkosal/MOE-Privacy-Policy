MOE - Privacy Policy

Camera and Photos Permission

MOE application may request access to the camera and photo storage on your device. This section outlines how and why these permissions are used:

1. Purpose of Camera and Photos Access

MOE application requires access to the camera and photo storage to allow users to upload personal files, including images and documents, within the app. This feature enables you to capture photos or select existing files from your device for various purposes, such as attaching images to notes or uploading documents for collaboration.

2. User Consent

Before accessing the camera or photo storage, MOE App will explicitly request your consent. You will be prompted to grant permission during the installation process or when attempting to use features that require these permissions.

3. Data Handling

All photos and documents captured or selected using the camera and photo access permissions are stored on your device and strictly for personal use.

4. Security Measures

App takes measures to ensure the security and privacy of files accessed through the camera and photo permissions. These files are stored with encryption on your device, and access is limited to the functionalities provided by the app.

5. Revoking Permissions

You have the right to revoke camera and photo access permissions at any time. This can typically be done through your device's settings. However, please note that revoking these permissions may limit certain features within the app that rely on accessing the camera or photo storage.

6. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to the camera and photos access policy will be reflected in the app's updated privacy policy. Users will be notified of any changes, and continued use of the app after such modifications constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
